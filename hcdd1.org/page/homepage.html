Hidalgo County Drainage District No. 1 (HCDD1) owns, manages, and maintains 256.30 miles of drainage systems, and maintains an additional 328.16 miles of drainage systems through inter-local agreements with surrounding entities. Currently HCDD1 is responsible for maintaining 584.46 miles of drainage systems, and is in the planning phase to construct an additional 81.42 miles. The grand total of current and future miles is 665.89,  equivalent to the distance it takes to travel from McAllen, Texas to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.
