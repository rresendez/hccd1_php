The North Branch Drain is a drainage system located in Precinct No. 4.  This system commences at approximately ¼ mile west of Cesar Chavez Road and approximately ¼ mile north of Hidalgo County Mile 17 ½ and travels in an easterly and northerly direction with an ultimate outfall into the J-02-00 Drain (Monte Cristo Drain).  HCDD No. 1 has improved road crossings at Mile 17 ½ Road, Terry Road and Tower Road for this system.  Plans have been prepared for the road crossings at Sharp Road, Irrigation Line and Mile 18.  All plans were prepared by HCDD No. 1 Engineering and road crossings at Mile 17 ½ and Terry Road were performed by HCDD No. 1 staff.  The Tower Road crossing was constructed under the HCDD No. 1 2012 Bond Rural Drainage Projects – Mile 17 ½ and Tower Area Road Drainage Improvement Project.

The subdivisions and the surrounding areas will benefit from the existing and proposed improvements by providing an improved outfall for the storm water to move out of the area.

Project plans are approximately 40% complete.
